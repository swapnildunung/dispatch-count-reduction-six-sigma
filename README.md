# 📈 Dispatch Count Reduction Using Six Sigma DMAIC

A Six Sigma Green Belt project focused on reducing unnecessary technician dispatches by improving remote troubleshooting effectiveness, strengthening agent capabilities and standardizing support processes through the DMAIC methodology.

---

## Project Overview

This project aimed to minimize avoidable technician dispatches by enhancing remote support capabilities and improving operational efficiency. Through structured process analysis, root cause identification and targeted improvements, the project achieved measurable reductions in dispatch rates while improving customer support effectiveness.

---

## Business Problem

A high volume of unnecessary technician dispatches increased operational costs, delayed issue resolution and reduced customer satisfaction. Many dispatches could have been prevented through effective remote troubleshooting and standardized support practices.

---

## Objectives

- Reduce unnecessary technician dispatches.
- Improve remote issue resolution effectiveness.
- Increase First Call Resolution (FCR).
- Standardize troubleshooting practices.
- Establish sustainable process controls.

---

## DMAIC Methodology

### Define

- Defined project scope, objectives and key performance indicators (KPIs).
- Identified unnecessary technician dispatches as the primary improvement opportunity.

### Measure

- Collected baseline operational data.
- Audited customer calls and chat interactions.
- Measured dispatch rates and First Call Resolution (FCR).

### Analyze

- Performed Pareto Analysis to identify the primary contributors to unnecessary dispatches.
- Applied Fishbone Diagram and 5 Whys to determine root causes.
- Identified skill gaps, inconsistent troubleshooting and missed remote resolution opportunities.

### Improve

- Enhanced agent training programs.
- Conducted pilot testing of improved troubleshooting workflows.
- Standardized work instructions and remote troubleshooting procedures.

### Control

- Implemented operational dashboards.
- Established Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
- Monitored KPIs to sustain improvements.

---

## Key Business Outcomes

- Reduced unnecessary technician dispatches by **15%**.
- Improved First Call Resolution (FCR) by **10%**.
- Increased consistency in remote troubleshooting.
- Reduced operational costs through better dispatch management.
- Improved overall customer support efficiency.
